How It Works

  1. Connect Instagram Account

    Link your Instagram business account to MuseumHub through Meta API.

  2. Receive Messages

    When customers send DMs to your Instagram account, they're automatically received by our system.

  3. AI Response

    Our chatbot processes the message and provides helpful responses about bookings, exhibits, and museum information.

  4. Manage Conversations

    View and manage all Instagram conversations from the Admin Portal.

Get Started

To enable Instagram integration, you need to:

  • Have an Instagram Business Account
  • Set up Meta API credentials
  • Configure webhook in Meta Developer Console
  • Enable the integration in Admin Portal
